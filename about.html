About Me

A Technical Foundation with a Community Heart

Background & Experience

I believe that the organizations making the most difference in our community deserve the highest quality support. My journey to founding Anacortes Nonprofit Navigator has been a 20-year path of combining technical expertise with a passion for local service.

With a Computer Science degree and an MBA, I spent the early part of my career as a consultant building complex systems for corporations. This phase of my life taught me the power of robust infrastructure and the efficiency that comes from well-designed technology.

After shifting my focus to raising my family and volunteering locally, I returned to the professional world with a new mission: serving the organizations that hold our community together.
