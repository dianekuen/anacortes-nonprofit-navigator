⚠️ Why the Navigation Was Restructured

🗂️ Too Many Top-Level Items

The original nav had 8+ top-level entries. Best practice for nonprofits is 5–6 max. The new structure uses 5 clear sections.

📰 Press Coverage Flooded the Nav

25+ individual press clips were listed as nav items. These now live on a single Press Coverage archive page under Our Work.

💰 Transparency Pages Were Scattered

Financials, Donation Tracker, and How It Works were three separate nav items. They're now unified under About as Accountability and How It Works.

🤝 No Clear "Support Us" Path

Donors had no obvious place to go. A dedicated Support Us section groups Ways to Give and Become a Sponsor together.

🎟️ Events Were Isolated

Film, Presentations, and Fundraiser were separate items. They now live as one Events page under Get Involved, where action-oriented visitors look.

📣 No Engagement Funnel

Get Involved is a new section that captures visitors ready to act — events, newsletter, and volunteering all in one place.

Original New Structure Why
Boots on the Ground Our Work — renamed; includes Our Partners (Ukrainian organizations receiving program funding) and Resilience Stories Clearer label; consolidated all mission-delivery content in one place
25 press clips in nav Our Work → Press Coverage — single archive page Nav is for navigation, not content. One page with all clips is easier to find and maintain.
